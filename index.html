About Us

Welcome to Borton Automotive Detailing LTD! We are a veteran owned and operated automotive detailing company. We serve the Boardman, OH and surrounding areas and strive to give the best services for the best prices!

We have packages for all size vehicles and can work with you specifically for your needs. After hour drop offs are available and all veterans receive a 10% discount with proof of ID or DD-214. Your satisfaction guaranteed.

Our Services

What we are best at - expert automotive detailing

Interior Detailing

We can help you clean your interiors to help remove stains, dirt, and other debris from daily use. Our interior cleaning includes cleaning and vacuuming of floor mats, dashboards, consoles, vents, steering wheels, and much more.

Exterior Detailing

Keep your vehicle looking brand new with our exterior cleaning services. For our exterior cleaning, we include a foam cannon wash, bug and tar removal, wax (or sealant), wheels and tire cleaning, window and glass cleaning, and more.

Maintenance Wash

Keep your vehicle looking brand new with our maintenance wash. Our experienced staff will arrive quickly and get the job done right, every time.

Full Detail Service

For cars, trucks and other vehicles we offer full service detailing to make your vehicle look new again! Interior and exterior detailing available.

Ceramic Coating

Innovative technology allows us to apply a 9H ceramic coating to help protect your vehicle from bird etchings, water spotting, and harsh environmental elements leaving a high gloss finish with unmatched water beading technology. Protect your investment and keep your vehicle looking better than when you bought it.

Paint Protection

We can protect your vehicle's paint from everyday damages including scuffs, scratches, flying rocks, and more. We do this by applying a layer of clear Paint Protection Film on the exterior of your vehicle.
